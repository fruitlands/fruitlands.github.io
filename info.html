The Speargrass Inn

The Speargrass Inn bed and breakfast is a restored building created by Mr. H McKenzie in 1871 with the intent of it being a hotel, named the Speargrass Hotel. Over the course of 30 years, the hotel changed ownership a number of times before it fell into a state of disrepair - until 1986 when a gigantic restoration project was intitatied, tranforming the building into the Fruitlands Gallery and Cafe, until ownership changed one last time, turning the cafe into the Speargrass Inn.
